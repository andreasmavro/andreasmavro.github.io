Simulation Methods

Monte Carlo Integration

In this short project, I investigate the Monte Carlo integration method for approximating the area under a curve. I compare the results with the exact value of the integrals and show that after a certain threshold, increase the number of simulations does not seem to be the optimal way to improve accuracy.
